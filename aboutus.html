ABOUT US

Webmaster: Jhandie A. Sumihay

Welcome to Tara Tea!, I’m Jhandie A. Sumihay, a 20-year-old from Barangay Lindaban, and I’m excited to share my passion for tea and community with you. Tara Tea is more than just a website; it's a celebration of the soothing moments and rich traditions that tea brings into our lives.

Here, you’ll find everything from tea reviews and brewing tips to stories that connect us to our roots. My goal is to create a warm and inviting space where tea lovers can come together, learn, and share their experiences.

Join me on this flavorful journey as we explore the world of tea, one cup at a time. Thank you for being a part of Tara Tea.

"let’s brew some wonderful memories together!"
